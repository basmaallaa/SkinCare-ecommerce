How do I choose the right product?

+
To pick the right skincare product:

- Know your skin type: oily, dry, combination, or sensitive.
- Identify your goal: hydration, anti-aging, acne control, brightening, etc.
- Check ingredients: avoid ingredients you’re allergic to; look for active ingredients suited for your concerns.
- Read product descriptions & reviews: they often indicate what skin type it works best for.
- Start small: try travel sizes or testers if available before committing to full-size products.
